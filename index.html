What makes us different.

01

The best of nature's hydration, in one can

02

Cleaner than clean

No artificial sweeteners, and DEFINITELY no salt overload masquerading as "isotonic." Just simple ingredients, sea salt, and magnesium malate - a more bioavailable form of magnesium. Good questions

FAQ

Why did you build Athleton?

Honestly, frustration. Every other electrolyte drink either upset our stomachs or left us thirstier than when we started, because so many pass off excess salt and sugar as "isotonic" and somehow good for you. We got tired of it and built a cleaner, better-for-you version, one we feel good drinking every day, and one we are happy to hand our kids too.

What makes Athleton different?

We are one of the very few hydration drinks built on both coconut water and prickly pear, with electrolytes and antioxidants from real foods. No artificial anything. Built for athletic culture, not the wellness aisle.

What is NOT in it?

No artificial sweeteners, no stevia or monk fruit, no cane sugar or dextrose, no citrates, no added potassium, no artificial dyes, no caffeine. The only sweetness comes from natural sources, never artificial.

What flavors are there?

Three to start: Strawberry Peach, Orange Yuzu, and Berry Berry Pear. More on the way. Waitlist members hear about new drops first.

How many calories and what is in each can?

Every flavor has its own recipe, so the exact calories and full ingredient list are printed right on each can.

Is it good for kids and youth athletes?

Yes! It is caffeine-free with no artificial ingredients, so it is clean enough for the whole sideline. We are parents ourselves, with kids both young and older, and we would not dream of handing them what is on shelves today. Athleton is exactly the drink we want in our own kids' hands, which is the whole reason it exists. If you have specific concerns, always check with your pediatrician about what is right for your child.

Why coconut water and prickly pear together?

Coconut water gives smooth, natural hydration. Prickly pear brings antioxidants. Together they do more than either alone, a pairing you will not find in many places.

Is it vegan, gluten-free, and non-GMO?

Yes to all three, plus Halal and Kosher certified.

When and where can I get it?

We are in pre-launch. Join the waitlist for first access. Direct from us first, with retail to follow.
