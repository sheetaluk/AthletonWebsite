Coconut water and prickly pear together provide potassium, magnesium, sodium, and chloride naturally. Coconut water is naturally lower in sodium, so we add sea salt to complete the profile. That's the full electrolyte story nothing from a lab.

Monk fruit is derived from a real fruit and has no sugar spike. Just enough to complement the natural sweetness already there.
