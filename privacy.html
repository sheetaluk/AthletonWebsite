Privacy, the clean way.

Last updated: May 25, 2026

We care about what goes into your body, and about what happens to your information. Here is exactly how we handle it, in plain language, no fine-print games.

The short version

  • We only collect what we actually need.
  • We use it to tell you about our launch and early access.
  • We never sell your data. Ever.
  • You can unsubscribe or ask us to delete your info anytime.

What we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address. That is all we need to let you know when Athleton arrives.

If you say yes to cookies, we also gather your approximate location (estimated from your IP address) and basic information about how you use the site, through our email platform, Klaviyo. This simply helps us send updates that are relevant to you rather than generic. We do not ask for sensitive personal information.

How we use it

  • To let you know when Athleton launches and to share early access and news.
  • To understand what people are excited about, so we can build a better product and better updates.
  • That is genuinely it.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data, and we do not hand it to advertisers. To run our signups and emails, we use a trusted partner, Klaviyo, which processes your information on our behalf and under its own strict privacy commitments. If that ever changes, we will update this page.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from any email with one click.
  • Decline cookies using the banner on our site. Everything still works.
  • Reach out anytime to see or delete the information we hold about you.

Cookies

We use only a small number of cookies, and only if you agree, to keep our updates relevant and understand what is resonating. You are always in control, and you can change your mind whenever you like.

Children

Athleton celebrates youth sports, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 18, please have a parent or guardian sign up and manage your information for you.

Keeping it safe

We use reasonable, industry-standard measures to protect your information. No method of storage is ever perfectly secure, but we take real care with what you trust us with.

Changes to this policy

If we update how we handle your information, we will refresh this page and update the date at the top. Nothing sneaky.

Get in touch

Questions about your privacy? Email us at hello@athletonofficial.com and a real human will help.
